"Herring_mean_weight_25-29_32.csv"
	- Yearly mean weight-at-age (in kg) of herring at ages 0,1, ...,7 and year 8 and older in ICES sea regions 25-29 and 32
	- 1974-2024
	- Data retrieved from the  2024 ICES report of BALTIC FISHERIES ASSESSMENT WORKING GROUP (WGBFAS)


"Herring_mean_weight_GoB.csv"
	- Yearly mean weight-at-age (in g) of herring at ages 0,1, ...,14 and year 15 and older in the Bothnian Bay (ICES sea regions 30 and 31)
	- 1974-2024
	- Data retrieved from the  2024 ICES report of BALTIC FISHERIES ASSESSMENT WORKING GROUP (WGBFAS)



"Herring_catch_in_numbers_25-29_32.csv"
	-Total yearly catch of herring in numbers at ages 0,1, ...,7 and year 8 and older in ICES sea regions 25-29 and 32
	- 1974-2024
	- Data retrieved from the  2024 ICES report of BALTIC FISHERIES ASSESSMENT WORKING GROUP (WGBFAS)



"Herring_catch_in_numbers_GoB.csv"
	-Total yearly catch of herring in numbers at ages 0,1, ...,14 and year 15 and older in the Bothnian Bay (ICES sea regions 30 and 31)
	- 1974-2024
	- Data retrieved from the  2024 ICES report of BALTIC FISHERIES ASSESSMENT WORKING GROUP (WGBFAS)
